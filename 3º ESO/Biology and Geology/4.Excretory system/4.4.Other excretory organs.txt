*Respiratory system*
Lungs excrete carbon dioxide during exhalation

*Liver*
It eliminates toxic substances, medicine, and bilirubin (produced during the breakdown of haemoglobin in the erythrocytes).
They are eliminated through bile, which flows into the small intestine.

*Sweat glands*
Twisted portion in the dermis. It is connected to a pore (where sweat is released) by duct. They are found all over in the skin.
Sweat is a fluid composed of water (95%), urea, uric acid, lactic acid, potassium, mineral salts.
It is also involved in temperature regulation: sweat cools the skin's surface when it evaporates.
