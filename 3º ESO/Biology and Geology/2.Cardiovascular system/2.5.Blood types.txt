*ABO system*
4 types: A, B, AB and O.
Types depend on the antigen on the red blood cell (A, B).
If the blood lacks of an antigen, it has antibodies that fight against those (anti-A, anti-B)

*Rhesus factor*
2 types: positive or negative.
Types depends on the antigen on the red blood cell (Rh).
If the blood lacks of the antigen Rh, it has antibodies that fight against it (anti-Rh).
