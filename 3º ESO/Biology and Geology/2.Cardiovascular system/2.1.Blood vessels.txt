There are 3 types of blood vessels:

*Artery*
It carries blood from the heart to the rest of the body.
It has thick walls with a strong muscle layer.
It branches into arterioles.

*Vein*
It carries blood from the rest of the body to the heart.
It has thinner and less elastic wallls.
It branches into venules.
