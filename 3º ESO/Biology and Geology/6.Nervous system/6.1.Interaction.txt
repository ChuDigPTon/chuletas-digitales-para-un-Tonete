It is one of the 3 vital functions. It allows us to perceive stimuli and respond to them. It coordinates the different organs and organ systems.
A stimuli is a change in environment or the body.

*Sensor receptors*
They perceive stimuli.
They are located in the sense organs.

*Nervous system*
It is formed of neurons.
It receives the stimuli and initiates a response. They connect sensors with effectors.
It coordinates and integrates all cells in the body.

*Effector organs*
-Endocrine system: formed of glands. Their response is the release of hormones, chemical messengers that travel through blood.
-Muscoskeletal system. formed of muscles and bones. Their response is movement (motor response).
