**Conditionals**

*Zero*
Form: If+present simple, present simple
Use: situations that are genrally true

*First*
Form: If+present simple, will+infinitive
Use: posible situations in the future

*Second*
Form: If+past simple, would+infinitive
Use: improbable siuations

*Third*
Form: If+past perfect, would+have+past participle
Use: improbable situations in the past


**Unless, as long as, provided/providing (that), in case**
Unless=if...not, except if
As long as, provided/providing (that)=if, only if
In case=because maybe
