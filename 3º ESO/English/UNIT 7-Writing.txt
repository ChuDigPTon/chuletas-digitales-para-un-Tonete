A REVIEW

*Paragraphs*
1: basic information of what you are reviewing
2: more detailed information (plot)
3: your opinion
4: your recommendation, conclusion

Use a variety of adjectives and adverbs (fascinating, thought-provoking, amazing, appealing, brilliant...)
Justify your opinions (for example, this is because, for instance, that is why)
Recomendation: I would recommend you to see/buy this because...
Use modifying adverbs (very, extremely, too...)

*Useful expresions*
Opinion: Personally, I think, As far as I'm concerned, In my opinion, I agree/disagree with...
To give conclusion: To sum up, In conclusion
